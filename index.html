About Us

Hospital consists of a network of walk in clinics throughout Kigali. These cover key towns upcountry and 5 centres within Kigali. This network is expanding and improving as part of our commitment to ensure that we provide the best primary care service in Rwanda.

We are bringing some of the clinics in major towns onto main streets where they are more accessible to our patients, and to expand the network to other key towns. Wherever you are in Uganda, you won’t be far from exceptional clinical care at an Hospital clinic.

  • Specialists are accessed on an appointment basis only following GP referral.
  • A pharmacy department that provides safe, quality and cost effective pharmaceutical care.
  • offers inpatient as well as outpatient services with a highly experienced team.
  • Open 7 days a week, the laboratory offers routine and specialized tests.

Hospital Introduces Free ECG Services to Promote Diagnosis of Heart Conditions

The ECG is a diagnostic test that assesses the electrical activity of the heart, which determines the effectiveness of heart function. Abnormal electrical activity is a pointer to underlying heart conditions such as Arrhythmias (irregular heartbeat), Heart Attacks, Coronary Heart Disease, Ischemic heart disease (condition when the arteries supplying the heart are narrowed).
