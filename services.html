General Checkup

Services are provided on a 24 hour basis, seven days per week, year round. “On-call” services are also provided on a daily basis to accommodate emergency cases. These services are provided by a highly qualified group of skilled Surgeons, Anaesthesiologists, Nursing and Support Staff.
